## Structure
- Opening paragraph: Hook and position interest
- Body paragraphs: Relevant experience and achievements
- Closing paragraph: Call to action and availability

## Content Guidelines
- Address hiring manager by name if possible
- Demonstrate company research
- Connect your experience to their needs
- Show enthusiasm and cultural fit
- Keep to one page maximum

## Tone and Style
- Professional yet personable
- Avoid generic templates
- Use specific examples
- Match company culture/voice
- Proofread carefully

## Human-Written Quality
- Vary sentence structure
- Use natural transitions
- Include personality
- Avoid overly formal language
- Be authentic and specific

## Company Overview
TechCorp is a fast-growing SaaS company focused on enterprise workflow automation. Founded in 2015, now 500+ employees across 3 offices.

## Culture and Values
- **Innovation-First**: Encourages experimentation and learning from failures
- **Collaboration**: Cross-functional teams, open communication
- **Work-Life Balance**: Flexible hours, remote-friendly
- **Diversity & Inclusion**: Strong commitment to diverse hiring

## Work Environment
- Agile development methodology
- Weekly tech talks and learning sessions
- Quarterly hackathons
- Modern tech stack with latest tools

## What They Value in Candidates
- Problem-solving abilities
- Team collaboration skills
- Continuous learning mindset
- Ownership and accountability
- Technical excellence with pragmatic approach
